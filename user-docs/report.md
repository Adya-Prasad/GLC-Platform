# ESG Reporting Guide

The GLC Platform automatically generates detailed ESG reports for your projects.

## 1. Accessing Reports

Navigate to **"Reports"** in the sidebar. You will see a list of generated reports for each of your loan applications.

## 2. Understanding the ESG Scorecard

The report provides a comprehensive breakdown of your ESG performance:

### Composite Score (0-100)
Calculated based on:
- **Completeness (20%)**: Do we have all required data?
- **Verifiability (25%)**: Is the data backed by documents?
- **GLP Alignment (25%)**: Does it meet international standards?
- **Penalties**: Points deducted for DNSH failures or Carbon Lock-in risks.

## 3. AI-Generated Insights

The report includes qualitative analysis from our AI agents:
- **Strengths**: What your project does well (e.g., "Strong biodiversity protection measures").
- **Gaps**: Missing information (e.g., "Lack of water usage data").
- **Recommendations**: Actionable steps to improve your score (e.g., "Upload third-party audit report").

## 4. Exporting

You can **Download PDF** to share this report with stakeholders, investors, or auditors.
