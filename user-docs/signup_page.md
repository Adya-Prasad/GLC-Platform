# User Login & Registration Guide

Welcome to the Green Lending Cycle (GLC) Platform! This guide will help you get started with creating an account and logging in securely.

## 1. Getting Started

To access the platform, navigate to the login page. You will see two primary options:

- **Enter as Borrower**: For companies seeking green loans.
- **Enter as Lender / Bank**: For financial institutions reviewing loan applications.

![Login Screen](/static/img/signup-page-screenshot.png)

## 2. Creating a New Account

We've simplified the registration process for the hackathon. You don't need email verification!

1. **Select your Role**: Click on either "Enter as Borrower" or "Enter as Lender".
2. **Enter Organization Name**: Type your company or bank name.
3. **Create a Passcode**: Enter a new **6-digit passcode**.
   - *Tip: Remember this passcode! You will need it to log in again.*
4. **Click Continue**: The system will automatically create your account and log you in.

## 3. Logging In (Existing Users)

If you already created an account:

1. **Select your Role**.
2. **Enter your Organization Name** (exactly as you created it).
3. **Enter your 6-digit Passcode**.
4. **Click Continue**.

> **Note**: You will see a "âœ… Welcome back!" message if your login is successful.

## 4. Troubleshooting

- **"Incorrect passcode" error**: Double-check you entered the correct 6 digits. If you forgot it, try creating a new account with a slightly different name (e.g., "Green Corp 2").
- **"Network Error"**: Ensure the server is running and you have an internet connection.

---

### Security Note
Your data is secured with your unique organization name and passcode combination. Please keep your passcode private.
